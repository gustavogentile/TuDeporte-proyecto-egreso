Carrot Omelette Stuffed with Turkey

Delicious and healthy dinner in less than ten minutes. Do you dare? Then, be sure to try this grated carrot omelette stuffed with turkey ham.

Ingredients

  • Servings: 2
  • Difficulty: Low

Fresh Ingredients:

  • 1 carrot
  • 2 slices of turkey ham

Pantry Ingredients:

  • 2 eggs
  • 1 tablespoon oat flour
  • 1 pinch of salt
  • 1 tablespoon olive oil

Step by Step

  1. Peel and grate a carrot.
  2. Crack the eggs into a bowl and beat them.
  3. Add the grated carrot and oat flour to the beaten egg, a little salt and mix everything.
  4. Pour a little oil into a frying pan. When it is hot, pour the previous mixture and cook over medium heat. When it starts to cook, spread the turkey slices on top. Using a spatula, carefully fold the tortilla in half.
  5. Cook over medium-low heat for a little while and turn it over with the help of the spatula. If it is easier for you, you can help yourself with a plate to turn it over (as if it were a potato omelette).

Tricks and Tips

If you want, you can add a handful of grated cheese on top of the turkey slices.

You can accompany the omelet with a salad or serve it inside a piece of bread.
