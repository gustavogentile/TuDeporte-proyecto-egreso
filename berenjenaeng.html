Turkish style eggplants

“We know how much you like eggplants so we have cooked this recipe with you in mind. You will see how delicious they are, the combination of spices gives a spectacular flavor to the dish.”

Ingredients

Difficulty: Low

Time: 30'

Servings: 2

Fresh ingredients

  • Eggplant: 1 unit
  • Leek: 1 unit
  • Garlic: 1 clove
  • Red pepper: 70 grams

Pantry ingredients

  • Olive oil: 1 tablespoon
  • Crushed tomato: 100 milliliters
  • Pine nut: 1/2 tablespoon
  • Cumin powder: 1 pinch
  • Nutmeg: 1 pinch
  • Cinnamon: 1 pinch
  • Raisin: 1 tablespoon

Steps

  1. Preheat the oven to 200ºC.
  2. Wash and cut the eggplant lengthwise in half. With a knife, make cuts in the meat in a grid pattern. Sprinkle them with a little oil and salt, and bake them for 30 minutes in the oven with heat up and down.
  3. Pour a few drops of olive oil in a frying pan and sauté the pine nuts until golden. Remove them and reserve.
  4. Meanwhile, chop the leek, garlic and pepper.
  5. In the same pan you used before, add a little more oil and sauté the chopped vegetables for 5 minutes.
  6. Add the crushed tomato, raisins and reserved pine nuts, cumin, nutmeg and cinnamon, and cook over low heat for 8 minutes.
  7. After the eggplants' baking time, take them out of the oven and carefully extract their meat. Add it to the pan containing the vegetables and spices, and cook for a few minutes so that the flavors integrate.
  8. Stuff the eggplants with the above and serve.
