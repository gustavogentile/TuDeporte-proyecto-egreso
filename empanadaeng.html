Empanadas with Whole Wheat Flour and Oats

Prepare some delicious healthy empanadas with a mixture of whole wheat flours and oats.

Ingredients

  • Time: 1 hour and 30 minutes approx.

For the Dough:

  • 1 cup whole wheat flour
  • 1 cup oat flour
  • 1 egg
  • c/n Water

For the Filling:

  • Chicken to taste
  • Morron to taste
  • Onion to taste
  • Condiments to taste

Step by Step

  1. Mix whole wheat flour and oat flour. Add the egg and mix it with water until forming the dough.
  2. For the Filling: Cook the minced chicken, bell pepper and onion with the seasonings and salt to taste. Dehydrate in a pan for a few minutes and add the cheese when the filling is cold, so it melts in the oven.
  3. Bake the empanadas until done and enjoy.
