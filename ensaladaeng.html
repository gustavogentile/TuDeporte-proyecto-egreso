Refreshing Salad

If you don't feel like turning on the stove today, you don't have to. Prepare this refreshing salad to eat, you just have to cut and mix. How easy!

Ingredients

  • 10 Ingredients
  • Difficulty:Low
  • Time: 10 minutes
  • Servings: 1

Fresh Ingredients

  • Avocado: 1/2 unit
  • Onion: 1/4 unit
  • Green pepper: 1/4 unit
  • Red pepper: 1/4 unit
  • Parsley: 1/2 pinch
  • Lemon: 1/4 unit

Pantry Ingredients

  • Canned tuna: 1/2 large can
  • Canned chickpea: 75 grams
  • Olive oil: 1/2 tablespoon
  • Salt: 1/2 pinch

Steps

  1. Very finely chop the onion and peppers.
  2. Drain the cooked chickpeas from their jar.
  3. Cut the avocado into pieces.
  4. Pour all of the above into a container. Add the drained tuna from its can and mix.
  5. Season with a little oil, lemon, salt and chopped parsley and the salad is ready!
