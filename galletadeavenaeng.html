Oatmeal and Banana Cookies

If you are one of those who likes to eat healthy and take great care of what cookies you put in your body, these oatmeal and banana cookies will surely pass your approval. And they are very simple!

Ingredients

  • 3 Ingredients
  • Difficulty: Low
  • Time: 25 minutes
  • Servings: 2

Fresh Ingredients

  • Banana: 1 unit

Pantry Ingredients

  • Oatmeal: 1/2 cup
  • Cinnamon: 1 teaspoon

Steps

  1. Peel the banana and, with a fork, mash it until puree.
  2. In a container, pour the mashed banana, add the oats and cinnamon. Mix all the ingredients well.
  3. With your hands, form balls and place them on baking paper on an oven-safe tray.
  4. Crush the balls a little (they will be cookie-shaped) and bake them at 200Â°C for 20 minutes.
  5. Remove from the oven, let them cool and serve on a plate. Now you can enjoy your oatmeal cookies!

Tricks and Tips

These oatmeal cookies may be less crunchy than others, but they are super healthy and delicious.

Homemade oatmeal cookies are a much healthier and more nutritious option than the typical cookies you will find in the supermarket, no matter how much they include oats or whole grains among their countless ingredients.

You can add nuts to the oatmeal cookies and, if you want, also a little honey.
