Lentil Burgers with Green Pepper

“Do you like lentil burgers? They are incredibly good and so easy to make that we hope that from now on, you will make them all the time. Delicious!”

    Ingredients

  • 8 ingredients
  • Difficulty: Low
  • Time: 20'
  • Servings: 2

Fresh ingredients

  • Green pepper: 1/2 unit
  • Lettuce: 120 grams
  • Cherry tomato: 6 units
  • Onion: 1/2 unit

Pantry ingredients

  • Canned lentil: 3/4 small jar
  • Cumin powder: 1 teaspoon
  • Salt: 1 pinch
  • Olive oil: 3 tablespoons

Steps

  1. In your food processor, place the onion and green pepper cut into thick pieces, the canned lentils, the cumin and a little salt. Blend a little until you get a dough with a semi-thick texture (don't over-shred the ingredients).
  2. Take a little of the mixture and shape it into a ball. Flatten it a little and achieve the shape of a hamburger. Repeat the operation until the dough is finished.
  3. Pour olive oil into a frying pan. When it is hot, fry the burgers.
  4. Place them on a plate and accompany them with a simple salad of lettuce and cherry tomatoes. Enjoy!
