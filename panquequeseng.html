Oatmeal and Banana Pancakes

Delicious oatmeal and banana pancakes, perfect for a healthy breakfast.

Ingredients

  • Time: 15 minutes
  • Servings: 1
  • 2 eggs
  • 1 cup of oats (flour or blended oats)
  • Pinch of cinnamon
  • Honey, sweetener or sugar to taste
  • Handful of nuts
  • 1/2 banana
  • 1 teaspoon baking powder

Step by Step

  1. Add your preferred sweetener, baking powder and cinnamon together in a container.
  2. Crush the banana and add it to the mixture along with the walnuts and oats.
  3. Finally, add the eggs and mix well. If the consistency is not very soft, you can add a splash of milk.
  4. Pour the mixture into a hot pan and cook until ready. Enjoy!
